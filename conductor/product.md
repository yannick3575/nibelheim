# Initial Concept
Nibelheim is a personal AI workspace designed for an enthusiast to discover and learn about AI technologies through automation, experimentation, and organization.

# Target Users
An AI enthusiast focused on continuous learning and discovering new AI capabilities and trends.

# Goals
- Automate technology watch to stay updated with the latest AI developments.
- Organize and refine a library of reusable prompts.
- Conduct stochastic experiments and simulations to understand AI model behaviors.

# Key Features
- **Tech Watch Dashboard:** Automated aggregation, summarization, and trend tracking of AI-related news and articles.
- **Prompt Library:** A system for organizing, testing, and versioning prompts across different models.
- **Stochastic Lab:** Tools for running simulations, comparing model outputs, and analyzing probabilistic distributions.
